£26,730.98

The minimum funding guarantee (opens in new tab) for newly opened mainstream academies is calculated by the local authority (LA). It ensures the change in per pupil school budget share between the previous academic year and current academic year will not fall below the LA’s guaranteed change. Your LA has set this at 0.5%.
