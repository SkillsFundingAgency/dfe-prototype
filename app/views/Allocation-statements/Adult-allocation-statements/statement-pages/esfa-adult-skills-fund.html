Changes to your allocation

You must follow the ESFA adult skills funding rules (opens in new tab). We can reduce or remove your allocation if there's evidence that public funds are at risk.

! Warning Allocation statements are not legally binding. They do not create any legal obligation against the Secretary of State for Education, acting through the Education and Skills Funding Agency (ESFA).

Submit a business case

You can submit a business case (opens in new tab) if you have given incorrect data to the ESFA and this has affected your funding allocation. For example, a mistake in your R04 return.
